Who We Are

Our mission is to guide people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ.

Vision

To win and properly disciple 1,000,000 souls to Christ in 21,000 schools in Nigeria and across Africa.

Mission

  • School Outreaches: Share the gospel, provide healing and deliverance, and inspire greatness in 1,000,000 teenagers by 2030.
  • School Fellowship: Establish TCAf fellowships in 21,000 schools across Nigeria and Africa.
  • School Discipleship: Distribute discipleship manuals to 1,000,000 students, promoting spiritual growth.
  • Campus Ministry: Spread the gospel through various methods, establish fellowships, and train leaders on campuses.
  • Charity & Academic Support: Offer tutorials, study materials, relief packages, and scholarships to 10,000 underprivileged students.
